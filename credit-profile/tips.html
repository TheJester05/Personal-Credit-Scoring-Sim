Credit Improvement Tips

  • ✅ Pay at least the minimum on all credit accounts on time
  • 📉 Keep credit utilization below 30% of your limit
  • 🕒 Maintain older credit lines to increase average account age
  • 🧾 Don’t apply for too many credit cards in a short period
  • 📊 Use a mix of credit types (installment + revolving)
  • 🔁 Review your credit report regularly to fix errors
