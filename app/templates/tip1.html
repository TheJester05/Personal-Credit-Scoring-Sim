✅ Pay at least the minimum on all credit accounts on time

Payment history is the most important part of your credit score, typically making up 35%. Even a single late or missed payment can drop your score significantly.

  • Set up automatic payments to avoid forgetting due dates.
  • Always pay at least the minimum amount — more is better.
  • Late payments can stay on your report for up to 7 years.
