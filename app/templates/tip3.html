🕒 Maintain older credit lines to increase average account age

Older accounts help build your credit age, which makes up about 15% of your credit score. Closing old accounts can shorten your credit history.

  • Keep older credit cards open, even if used sparingly.
  • Use them occasionally and pay them off to avoid inactivity.
