📊 Use a mix of credit types

Credit mix refers to having both revolving credit (credit cards) and installment loans (auto, mortgage, etc.). It counts for 10% of your score.

  • Don’t open unnecessary accounts just for mix.
  • But having both card and loan history can help long-term.
