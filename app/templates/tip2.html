📉 Keep credit utilization below 30%

Your credit utilization ratio is the percentage of your credit limit you're currently using. A lower ratio suggests you manage credit well.

  • Try to use less than 30% of your credit limit.
  • Pay off your balance early — before the statement date.
  • Consider increasing your credit limit to lower your ratio.
