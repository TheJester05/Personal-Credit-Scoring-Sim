🔁 Review your credit report regularly to fix errors

Credit reports may contain inaccuracies that hurt your score. Regularly reviewing them helps you catch and correct issues early.

  • You can get a free report from each bureau every 12 months at AnnualCreditReport.com.
  • Dispute incorrect info through the reporting agency.
