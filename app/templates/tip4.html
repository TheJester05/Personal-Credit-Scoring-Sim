🧾 Don’t apply for too many credit cards in a short period

Each application for credit can trigger a “hard inquiry” which may slightly lower your score. Too many in a short time can appear risky.

  • Limit applications to only when necessary.
  • Space out applications at least 6 months apart if possible.
